Welcome to High Seas, we are a organization dedicated to helping clear up the plastic that is polluting our ocean. High Seas makes trips into oceans, rivers, and streams to collect unwanted plastic and materials that have been discarded. We clean the waste and process it to make clothing that is imbued with plastic making them ecofriendly, reusable, and cheap. Clothing with plastic imbued into it can keep the fibers of the clothing strong and cleaning easier as stains no longer become tough and hardworking task to get out from the clothing.
Helping the economy and the environment is our goal here at High Seas. Cleaning our oceans and making them a lot more beautiful and helping restore them to their natural beauty is what we strive to do here at High Seas
