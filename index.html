Harmful plastics in the sea! A better environment?

Harmful plastics in the sea create a hazardous environment for oceanic creatures. This plastic sitting in the ocean can be removed to help clean the environment, provide a better place for sea-creatures to live, and when recycled, better for the economy and environment. Here at High Seas you will find that our mission is to make the ocean a better place for all living things to provide clothing made with plastics collected from the sea to help give back to communities and help spread the awareness of oceanic polluting

Plastic clothing? A way for a cleaner future!

High Seas prides itself on the work to help clean the pollution from oceans, rivers and streams that might be harmful to the environment for both the earth and the aquatic creatures that live in these environments. Collecting the plastic from these bodies of water, cleaning them and using them to make stronger and durable clothing is our mission here at High Seas. Wearing our clothing helps the environment and economy with our clothing being cheaper and more durable. if our apperel wears out or no longer fits sending it to the local recycling plant will help reuse the clothing for another purpose in its expansive life cycle.
